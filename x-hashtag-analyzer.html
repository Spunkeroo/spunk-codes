Hashtag Strategy Tips
The 3-3-3 Rule

Use 3 broad hashtags (100K+ posts), 3 niche hashtags (10K-100K posts), and 3 micro hashtags (under 10K posts) for optimal reach.

Best Practices

Place hashtags at the end of your tweet or in a reply. Use 2-5 hashtags per tweet on X. Avoid banned or spammy hashtags.
