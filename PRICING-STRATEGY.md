## 3. PRICING TIERS — DETAILED BREAKDOWN

This structure is modeled after the most successful freemium tool sites (Canva, SmallPDF, TinyPNG) but priced for the indie hacker/solo founder market.

### TIER 1: FREE (Forever)

**Price:** $0
**Purpose:** Traffic generation, SEO, viral loops, trust building

**What's included:**

- No signup required
- No data collection
- New free tools added regularly

**Why this works:**
- TinyPNG model: free tier drives 4.5M monthly visits
- SmallPDF model: 30M+ monthly users from free tier
- Free tools are your #1 SEO asset and lead generation engine
- Every free tool page = a landing page that ranks on Google
- Zero-friction entry means maximum top-of-funnel volume

**What stays free permanently:**

- Basic functionality of every tool
- Unlimited uses per day

---

### TIER 2: PRO

**Price:** $9/month or $69/year (save 36%, effectively $5.75/mo)
**Purpose:** Recurring revenue, ebook access, premium features

**Reasoning for $9/mo (not $19/mo):**

The current pricing page shows $19/mo. Research strongly suggests $9/mo will perform better for this market:

1. **$9 is the indie hacker impulse threshold.** At $19/mo, conversion drops significantly for solo founders who are watching every dollar. The mental gap between "free" and "$19" is enormous. The gap between "free" and "$9" is much smaller.

2. **Math favors lower price + higher conversion.** At $19/mo with 2% conversion on 10,000 monthly visitors = 200 customers = $3,800/mo. At $9/mo with 5% conversion (proven to be achievable at this price point) on 10,000 visitors = 500 customers = $4,500/mo. Lower price, higher revenue.

3. **Comparable pricing:** TinyPNG Pro is $39/year ($3.25/mo). Notion Personal Pro is $10/mo. Buffer free plan covers most needs, paid is $6/mo per channel. The $9/mo positions spunk.codes competitively.

4. **Annual plan at $69/year** ($5.75/mo effective) is the real revenue driver. The 36% discount is aggressive enough to push annual adoption. Industry data shows "save $39" framing converts better than "save 36%."

**What Pro includes:**
- Everything in Free
- All 45 exclusive/premium tools unlocked
- All 17+ ebooks (full access, downloadable)
- All future ebooks included
- Ad-free experience
- Priority access to new tools
- Advanced features on existing tools (export options, bulk processing, saved presets)
- Email support
- Early access to beta features

---

### TIER 3: LIFETIME

**Price:** $149 one-time (reduced from current $199)
**Purpose:** Cash injection, early adopter capture, community building

**Reasoning for $149 (not $199):**

1. **AppSumo data shows $49–$99 is the sweet spot for lifetime deals.** At $199, you're competing with annual subscriptions to much larger tools (Codecademy Pro is $239.88/year). At $149, you're in the "impulse purchase for serious buyers" zone.

2. **$149 = 16.5 months of Pro at $9/mo.** This means anyone who plans to use the tools for more than ~1.4 years sees clear value. Given that tools are used indefinitely, this is an easy sell.

3. **$149 is the price of ~2 technical books on Amazon + a few months of any SaaS.** It feels like a steal for lifetime access to 240+ tools, 45 exclusive tools, and 17+ ebooks.

4. **Urgency pricing:** Launch at $99 for the first 100 customers (founding members), then raise to $149, with a stated plan to raise to $199 later. This creates FOMO and rewards early adopters.

**What Lifetime includes:**
- Everything in Pro, forever
- All current and future tools
- All current and future ebooks
- All current and future premium features
- Lifetime updates, no renewals
- Founding member badge (first 100 only)
- 1-on-1 onboarding call (first 50 only)
- Priority support forever
- Name in "Founding Members" section on site
- Vote on future tool development

## 4. BUNDLE STRATEGY

Bundles serve buyers who do not want a subscription but want specific products. These are one-time purchases.

### Bundle Pricing Psychology

**Key principles applied:**

1. **Anchor pricing:** Show the "if purchased separately" price crossed out next to the bundle price. Research shows this increases perceived value by 32%.

2. **Decoy effect:** The "Full Library" bundle at $49 makes the "Solo Founder Pack" at $19 look like a no-brainer, while making the Full Library look like the "smart" choice. Always include three options.

3. **Charm pricing:** Use .99 endings ($19.99, $29.99, $49.99). Studies consistently show these convert better than round numbers for products under $100.

4. **Save percentage:** Always show "Save XX%" on bundles. Anything over 40% feels like a deal.

## KEY STRATEGIC DECISIONS SUMMARY

| Decision | Recommendation | Reasoning |
|----------|---------------|-----------|
| Pro price | $9/mo (not $19) | Higher conversion at lower price = more total revenue. $9 is the indie hacker sweet spot. |
| Annual price | $69/year | 36% discount. "Save $39" framing. Drives annual commitments. |
| Lifetime price | $149 ($99 launch) | AppSumo sweet spot. 16.5 months of Pro value. FOMO with rising price. |
| Free tier | Keep ALL 240+ tools free | They are your SEO moat and top-of-funnel engine. Never paywall them. |
| Ebook model | Individual + bundles | $9.99 standard, $14.99 premium, $49.99 full library. Free chapter previews. |
| Payment processor | Stripe or LemonSqueezy | Stripe = 2.9% + $0.30. LemonSqueezy = 5% but handles VAT/tax globally. |
| Email platform | ConvertKit (Kit) | Free to 10,000 subs. Built for creators. 30% affiliate commission. |
| Social automation | Buffer (free) then SocialBee | Start free. Scale to paid when revenue justifies it. |
| Ads | Carbon Ads (free users only) | Developer-focused. Non-intrusive. Pro users get ad-free. |
| Affiliate focus | SEMrush, DigitalOcean, Hostinger, ConvertKit | High commission, relevant audience, strong cookie durations. |
