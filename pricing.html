Frequently Asked Questions

Are the free tools really free forever? ▼
Yes, all 300+ free tools remain free forever with no signup, no credit card, and no time limits. We believe in free-first. You also get community access and basic ebooks at no cost. Pro adds 75 exclusive premium tools, all 22 premium ebooks, early access, and an ad-free experience on top.
What are the 75 exclusive pro tools? ▼
Pro tools include advanced SEO analyzers, AI writing assistants, code review tools, premium API testers, advanced automation builders, and more. These are built specifically for professional developers and agencies who need power features not available in free alternatives.
Why did the price change from $9/mo to $19/mo? ▼
We've more than doubled the value. Pro originally included fewer tools at $9/mo. Now you get 75 exclusive premium tools (up from the original set), all 22 ebooks ($400+ value), early access to new tools, and priority updates. The new pricing reflects significantly expanded value. We've also added White-Label Reseller and Enterprise tiers for agencies and larger organizations.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Absolutely. Monthly and annual plans (Pro, Team, Enterprise) can be cancelled anytime. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. The Lifetime and White-Label plans are one-time payments with no recurring charges at all.
How does the 30-day money-back guarantee work? ▼
If you're not satisfied with any paid plan within 30 days of purchase, email us and we'll issue a full refund immediately. No questions asked, no hoops to jump through. Your free tools remain accessible regardless.
What's included in the 22 ebooks? ▼
Pro, Lifetime, and White-Label members get all current and future ebooks. Titles include "The 120-Site Empire Blueprint," "The AI Automation Playbook," "Building AI Agents," "The MCP Server Guide," and 18 more. Over $400 in total value, all included with your plan.
Is the Lifetime deal really limited to 500 members? ▼
Yes. We're keeping Lifetime membership limited to 500 founding members at the $349 price. Only 127 of 500 spots remain. Once those spots fill, the price will increase significantly or the plan may be removed entirely. Early supporters get the best deal.
What is the White-Label Reseller plan? ▼
The White-Label Reseller plan ($999, one-time) lets you resell all 300+ free tools, 75 premium tools, and 22 ebooks under your own brand. We remove all SPUNK.CODES branding, you add your logo and domain, and you keep 100% of the revenue. Lifetime updates and a commercial license are included. Perfect for agencies and entrepreneurs building their own SaaS offering.
What does the Enterprise plan include? ▼
Enterprise ($249/mo or $1,999/yr) is built for larger organizations. It includes unlimited seats, full API access, custom integrations, a dedicated account manager, SLA with uptime guarantees, custom branding options, priority feature requests, and priority onboarding. Contact us for custom needs.
How does the Team plan work? ▼
The Team plan ($49/mo or $399/yr) covers up to 10 members under one subscription. You get a team admin dashboard to manage members, white-label exports with your own branding, and priority support. Perfect for agencies and dev teams. Need more than 10 seats? Check out the Enterprise plan.
Do you offer affiliate commissions? ▼
Yes! Our affiliate program pays 30% recurring commission on all referrals with a 90-day cookie window. Join the affiliate program here.
