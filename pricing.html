Frequently Asked Questions

Are the tools really free? +
Yes, 100%. All 200+ browser tools are free forever with no signup, no account, and no data collection. They run entirely in your browser. We will never charge for them or put them behind a paywall.
What do I get by upgrading to Pro? +
Pro gives you access to all premium ebooks, advanced tool features (like bulk exports, custom templates, and API access), an ad-free experience, early access to new tools, and email support. It is for power users who want the full experience.
What is the difference between Pro and Lifetime? +
Pro is a subscription ($19/mo or $99/year). Lifetime is a one-time $199 payment that gives you everything in Pro forever, plus all future ebooks, lifetime updates, and a 1-on-1 onboarding call. If you plan to use the tools for more than 10 months, Lifetime saves you money.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime? +
Yes, cancel anytime with one click. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no hoops to jump through.
Do bundles include Pro access? +
The Solo Founder Pack, Creator Pack, and Social Growth Pack include premium tool features. The Full Library includes all ebooks. The Everything Bundle includes all ebooks plus lifetime Pro access -- it is the best deal if you want everything.
Is there a money-back guarantee? +
Yes. Every paid plan and bundle comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied for any reason, contact us and we will issue a full refund, no questions asked.
Can I switch plans later? +
Absolutely. You can upgrade from Free to Pro, from Pro monthly to Pro annual, or from Pro to Lifetime at any time. If upgrading from Pro to Lifetime, we will credit your remaining Pro subscription.
Do you offer team or enterprise pricing? +
Not yet, but we are working on it. For now, DM us on X @SpunkArt13 or email hello@spunk.codes for bulk pricing.
