Money-Saving Grocery Tips

Buy Store Brand

Store brands are 20-30% cheaper than name brands and often made in the same factory.

Shop Seasonal Produce

In-season fruits and veggies cost 50% less. Check what's in season this month.

Buy in Bulk (Wisely)

Rice, beans, oats, and pasta store well. Only buy perishables in bulk if you'll use them.

Never Shop Hungry

Studies show hungry shoppers spend 64% more on average. Eat before you go.

Use a List (This One!)

Shoppers with lists spend 23% less. Stick to it and avoid impulse buys.

Compare Unit Prices

Check the price per oz/lb on shelf tags. Bigger packages aren't always cheaper per unit.

Frozen is Fine

Frozen fruits, veggies, and fish are flash-frozen at peak freshness and cost less.

Plan Your Meals

Meal planning reduces food waste by 25% and ensures you only buy what you need.
