Implementation Roadmap

Do not try to switch everything at once. Here is the order we recommend:

Week 1: Quick Wins (Saves $200+/month)

  1. Cancel stock photo subscription → switch to Unsplash/Pexels
  2. Cancel Canva Pro → use Canva Free + SpunkArt generators
  3. Cancel password manager → switch to Bitwarden Free
  4. Cancel AI writing tool → use ChatGPT Free + SpunkArt AI Writer

Week 2: Design & Dev (Saves $300+/month)

  1. Cancel Adobe Creative Cloud → switch to Photopea + DaVinci Resolve
  2. Cancel paid hosting → migrate to Cloudflare Pages or GitHub Pages
  3. Cancel Copilot → install Codeium
  4. Cancel Postman paid → switch to Hoppscotch

Week 3: Marketing & Business (Saves $300+/month)

  1. Cancel SEO tools → switch to Google Search Console + SpunkArt SEO tools
  2. Cancel email marketing paid tier → switch to Brevo Free or MailerLite Free
  3. Cancel project management → switch to ClickUp Free
  4. Cancel accounting → switch to Wave

Week 4: Communication (Saves $100+/month)

  1. Cancel Zoom Pro → switch to Google Meet + Jitsi
  2. Cancel Calendly Pro → switch to Cal.com or Calendly Free
  3. Cancel Slack Pro → evaluate if Slack Free or Discord is sufficient

Before You Cancel: Export Your Data

Always export your data from paid tools before canceling. Most SaaS tools allow data export (Settings → Export or Account → Download Data). Back up templates, campaigns, project data, and any content you have created in the paid tool.
