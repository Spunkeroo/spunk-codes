Frequently Asked Questions

Can employees access this themselves?

The current version is designed for HR managers and team leads. It's a single-user tool where one person manages the employee database. Self-service employee portals may be added in a future update.

Does it handle payroll?

This tool focuses on employee management, leave tracking, onboarding, and reviews. For payroll, you'll need a dedicated service. The HR Bot pairs well with the Expense Tracker for tracking payroll expenses.

Is employee data secure?

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage and never leaves your device. There's no server, no cloud storage, and no account to hack. It's the most private HR tool possible.

How many employees can it handle?

There's no artificial limit. localStorage typically supports 5-10MB of data, which is enough for hundreds of employees with full leave and review histories.
