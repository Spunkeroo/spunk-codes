Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data really private?

Yes. All tools use browser localStorage. Your data never leaves your device. There are no accounts, no servers, and no tracking of your business data. The only analytics we use (Google Analytics and Clarity) track page visits, not your tool data.

What happens if I clear my browser data?

Your tool data is stored in localStorage. If you clear all browser data, you will lose your saved information. We recommend using the CSV export feature regularly to back up important data.

Can I use these tools on mobile?

Yes. All tools are fully responsive and work on phones, tablets, and desktops. The interfaces adapt to smaller screens automatically.

Are there any limits?

No artificial limits. You can add as many clients, deals, employees, invoices, or tasks as you want. The only limit is your browser's localStorage capacity (typically 5-10MB, enough for thousands of entries).

How do the connected tool integrations work?

The Analytics Dashboard reads data from other tools via localStorage keys. When you use the CRM, Pipeline, HR Bot, or any other tool, they save data locally. The dashboard detects this data and displays aggregated metrics. You can toggle which tools to connect in the settings panel.
