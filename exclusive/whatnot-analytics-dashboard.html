💡 Tips & Best Practices

TIMING Best times to go live on Whatnot: Weekday evenings (7-10 PM local) and weekend afternoons (1-5 PM) consistently draw the most viewers. Thursday and Sunday tend to perform best. Avoid Monday mornings and late nights past 11 PM.
DURATION Optimal show length: 1.5-2.5 hours is the sweet spot. Shows under 60 minutes don't build enough momentum. Shows over 3 hours see viewer drop-off. If you have 50+ items, consider splitting into two shows.
PRICING Pricing strategies that work: Start bids at 40-60% of market value to drive engagement. Use $1 start auctions for the first 3 items to hook viewers early. Place your best items at 25% and 75% through the show to maintain energy peaks.
ENGAGEMENT Viewer retention tactics: Call out buyers by name. Run flash giveaways every 20 minutes. Tease upcoming items ("Wait until you see what's next"). Ask questions to chat. The more interactive, the longer viewers stay.
GROWTH Growing your following: Go live on a consistent schedule (same day/time each week). Cross-promote on Instagram, TikTok, and X. Collaborate with other sellers for cross-streams. Every show should end with "Follow for the next drop."
