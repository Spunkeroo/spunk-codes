About Color Blindness Types:
Protanopia (~1% of males): Reduced sensitivity to red light.
Deuteranopia (~1% of males): Reduced sensitivity to green light. Most common type.
Tritanopia (~0.003%): Reduced sensitivity to blue light. Very rare.
Achromatopsia (~0.003%): Complete color blindness, seeing only shades of gray.
