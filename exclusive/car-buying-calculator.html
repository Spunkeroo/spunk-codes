💡 Trade-In Tips

Get Multiple Offers

Check CarMax, Carvana, and KBB Instant Cash Offer before visiting a dealer. Having competing offers gives you leverage.

Negotiate Separately

Never reveal your trade-in until after negotiating the new car price. Dealers use trade-in value to disguise bad deals on the new car.

Clean It Up

A detailed car can get 5-15% more. Spend $150 on detailing to potentially gain $500-1500 on trade-in value.

Fix the Easy Stuff

Replace burnt-out lights, fix minor dents, and address any check engine lights before trading in. Small fixes = bigger offers.

💪 Negotiate Like a Pro

Know the dealer's real cost and use it to negotiate a fair deal. The sticker price is never the final price.
