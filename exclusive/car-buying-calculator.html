💬 Negotiation Scripts & Tips

Use these proven scripts and strategies to save thousands at the dealership.

Best Months to Buy

December (year-end quotas), October (new model year arrivals), end of any quarter (March, June, September). Monday mornings are the best day/time -- dealers are hungry after a slow weekend.

Opening Line

"I have been researching this vehicle online and I know the invoice price. I am ready to buy today if we can agree on a fair price. What is your best out-the-door number?"

Never Discuss Monthly Payments First

Always negotiate the total price, NOT the monthly payment. Dealers love stretching terms to make payments look small while you pay more overall. Say: "Let us focus on the out-the-door price first, then we will talk about financing."

The Walk-Away

"Thank you for your time. That number is not going to work for me. Here is my card -- if you can come closer to [your target], give me a call." Then walk out. 70% of the time they will call you back within 24 hours with a better offer.

Fair Price Range

Target 3-5% below MSRP for popular models, 8-12% below MSRP for slow sellers. Check Edmunds, KBB, and TrueCar for the fair purchase price in your ZIP code before you go.

Trade-In Strategy

Never mention your trade-in until AFTER you have agreed on the purchase price of the new vehicle. Get offers from CarMax, Carvana, and Vroom first so you have competing bids.

Financing Trick

Get pre-approved at your bank or credit union BEFORE visiting the dealer. This gives you leverage. Let the dealer try to beat your rate, but never accept a higher one. Say: "I am already pre-approved at X%. Can you beat that?"

Refuse Add-Ons

Say NO to: paint protection, fabric protection, VIN etching, extended warranties (buy third-party later if needed), nitrogen tire fill, pinstriping. These have 200-500% markups. Say: "No thank you, I would just like the vehicle."

The Email Strategy

Email 5-10 dealers requesting their best out-the-door price on the exact vehicle you want (include stock number if possible). Play them against each other. "Dealer X offered me $31,200 OTD. Can you beat that?"

Final Check

Before signing, verify: out-the-door price matches verbal agreement, no added fees you did not discuss, interest rate matches what was quoted, loan term is correct, and all agreed-upon items are in writing.
