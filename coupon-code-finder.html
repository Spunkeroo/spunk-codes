Cashback Stacking Strategy

Stack multiple discounts on a single purchase. Here's how to maximize savings:

1

Start with a Cashback Portal

Go through Rakuten, TopCashback, or your credit card's shopping portal before buying. This gets you 1-15% back.

2

Apply a Coupon Code

Use Honey or Capital One Shopping to auto-apply the best coupon code at checkout. Or try the generic codes above.

3

Pay with a Cashback Credit Card

Use a credit card that gives 2-5% cashback on the purchase category. This stacks on top of portal cashback.

4

Buy Discounted Gift Cards First

Buy gift cards at 5-15% off from Raise, CardCash, or your credit card rewards portal, then use them to pay.

5

Submit Receipt for More Cashback

Scan your receipt in Ibotta, Fetch Rewards, or Receipt Hog for additional cashback on eligible items.

Total savings: 15-40% off almost any purchase by stacking all 5 layers

10 Tips for Finding Unadvertised Discounts

  • 1Abandon your cart — Add items, leave. Many stores email you a 10-15% off code within 24 hours.
  • 2Sign up for email lists — Most stores give 10-20% off your first order for joining their email list.
  • 3Use incognito mode — Some sites show higher prices to returning visitors. Incognito resets your profile.
  • 4Check the app — Many retailers offer app-only coupons and discounts (Target Circle, Walmart app, etc.).
  • 5Ask for student/military discount — Even if not advertised, many stores offer 10-15% for students and military. Just ask.
  • 6Price match — Walmart, Target, Best Buy, and many others price match competitors. Ask at checkout.
  • 7Buy refurbished — Apple, Amazon, Best Buy sell certified refurbished items at 15-40% off with warranties.
  • 8Chat with customer service — Ask for discounts via live chat. Agents often have promo codes they can apply.
  • 9Follow on social media — Stores often share exclusive deals and flash sales on their social media pages.
  • 10Time your purchase — Shop on Tuesdays (new sales launch), end of season (clearance), or end of month (sales quotas).
